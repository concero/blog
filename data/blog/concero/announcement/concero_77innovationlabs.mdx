We are excited to announce an innovative collaboration between Concero and 77 Innovation Labs, uniting two pioneering forces in the blockchain ecosystem to accelerate the development and adoption of secure, scalable Web3 solutions.

## Revolutionising Blockchain Infrastructure together

This strategic partnership combines Concero's cutting-edge cross-chain communication platform with 77 Innovation Labs' extensive expertise in blockchain security and education. As a participant in the Chainlink BUILD program, Concero has established itself as a leader in blockchain interoperability, offering sub-minute cross-chain transfers while maintaining unwavering security standards. 77 Innovation Labs, renowned for their specialised Chainlink solutions and commitment to blockchain security, brings their wealth of experience in solving real-world connectivity challenges.

## Shared vision for the future

Our collaboration is founded on a shared commitment to advancing the blockchain ecosystem through:

1. Enhanced Security: Leveraging 77 Innovation Labs' security expertise alongside Concero's robust infrastructure to establish new standards in cross-chain security.
2. Educational Leadership: Combining our resources to promote blockchain education and adoption through hands-on experience and practical implementation.
3. Technical Innovation: Merging our technical capabilities to develop more efficient, secure, and user-friendly blockchain solutions.

## What This Means for the Web3 Community

Developers and projects building on Web3 can look forward to:

- Advanced cross-chain development tools with enhanced security features
- Comprehensive educational resources and technical support
- Improved scalability and interoperability solutions
- Access to combined expertise in Chainlink technologies and blockchain security

This partnership marks a significant milestone in our journey to create a more connected and secure blockchain ecosystem. Together, we are committed to driving innovation while maintaining the highest standards of security and usability in the Web3 space.

Stay tuned for upcoming announcements about joint initiatives, new product features, and educational programs that will emerge from this collaboration.

## About 77 Innovation Labs

77 Innovation Labs is a startup created to address the two main challenges of the blockchain ecosystem: Education and Security. Our team consists of specialists who have solved real-world challenges, with internationally recognised expertise in connectivity and blockchain security solutions, being pioneers in specialised Chainlink solutions.

## About Concero

Concero is a pioneering blockchain infrastructure platform focused on enabling seamless cross-chain communication and interoperability. As a participant in the Chainlink BUILD program, Concero has developed a fully decentralised protocol that facilitates secure value and message transfers between different blockchain networks in under a minute. Concero provides developers with essential tools for building cross-chain applications while maintaining high-security standards and complete decentralisation.
